Hello again! In this post, we'll do something really boring... but I'll try to make it interesting (or not). I'm talking about practising drawing and shading the basic shapes and forms: bended planes, the iconic sphere, cones, boxes... You name it.

I'm not going to lie. This exercise can be tedious and pretty simplistic in a painfully boring way, however, if it is that simple then we can prove we can do it, right? (No skipping and no cheating, cheeky artist). Jokes aside, it's important to check on this skill because it influences us greatly how well we perform while constructing our models, let that be people, animals, objects or buildings.

If we want to really improve I dearly suggest to go through this step first. I promise, everything I write is from experience. So, for today or the next few days, let's try to complete a full A4 page.
